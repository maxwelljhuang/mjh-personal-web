About Me

My Background

I'm currently studying Information Systems and Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I have experience in full-stack development using technologies like Svelte/Typescript, Django/Python, and PostgreSQL.

I'm passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems, particularly in areas like healthcare efficiency and sustainability.

My Projects & Experience

I've worked on various projects, including research at Carnegie Mellon University, developing a reusable Python package for manipulating time-series data aimed at improving healthcare efficiency.

I'm also a part-time full-stack developer at LearnSuite AI, working on feature development for student management and student/teacher portal, as well as AI Agents for scheduling with CrewAI.

Previously, I worked on a research project under Professor Lu at Santa Clara University for AI fairness/bias in automated resume filtering. The research paper titled "National Origin Discrimination in Deep-learning-powered Automated Resume Screening" was awarded "Best Paper Award" - ISAP 2022 at Copenhagen, Denmark.
